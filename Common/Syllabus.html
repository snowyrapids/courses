Tabooed Behaviors and Items

Student grades can be docked for unexcused absences, impolite or disruptive classroom behavior (including talking, especially when it is off-topic), lack of appropriate class participation, and inappropriate technology use. Ear-buds and headphones are not allowed without instructor approval. The student is expected to regularly bring a calculator, pencil/pen, and paper for taking notes. Cell phones in place of calculators are not permitted during quizzes, even when a calculator might be necessary.
