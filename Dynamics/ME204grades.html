ME 204 Grade Breakdown

Grades are made according to the following table:

Quizzes: 10% A: 93-100% A-: 90-92% B+: 87-89%
Attendance: 5% B: 83-86% B-: 80-82% C+: 77-79%
Homework: 10% C: 73-76% C-: 70-72% D+: 67-69%
Exams (total): 75% D: 63-66% D-: 60-62% F: Anything below 60%

Note: Anything discussed in class or covered by assigned reading/class assignments is "fair game" (could be used) for quizzes. The grade-category weights (quizzes, assignments, etc.) may be adjusted as the instructor sees fit.
