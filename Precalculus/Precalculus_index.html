Homework Assignments

Homework assignments should utilize the BYU-Idaho ME Department's homework standard insofar as it is applicable, particularly on story problems. This includes the following aspects:

  • One problem per page
  • Assignment/problem number, name, and date must be written at the top of the page along with the page number out of the total pages (e.g., "pp. 2/8")
  • Given/Find/Solution blocks
  • Engineering graph paper (applies only to handwritten work)
  • Clear handwriting and organization
  • Equation referencing, especially internally (equations you've made in your solution)
  • Notes and commenting ("document" your solution)
